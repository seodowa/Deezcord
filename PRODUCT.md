# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Deezcord's audience is basically for all people who want to communicate in real-time. This product's purpose is to be an open-source alternative to corporate constructed communication applications.

## Product Purpose
To provide an open-source alternative to corporate-constructed communication applications. Success looks like a reliable, real-time messaging platform that users trust for their daily communication needs.

## Brand Personality
Open Source Software

## Anti-references
Meta's Facebook Messenger

## Design Principles
1.  **Open & Accessible:** The UI should feel inviting and easy to understand for a broad audience, avoiding overly complex or "tech-heavy" patterns unless necessary.
2.  **Focus on the Conversation:** The chat interface should be the clear center of attention. Extraneous UI elements should not distract from the real-time communication.
3.  **Distinct from Corporate Clichés:** Avoid design patterns heavily associated with platforms like Facebook Messenger (e.g., specific chat bubble styles, cluttered feature sets) in favor of a cleaner, perhaps more community-focused aesthetic.
4.  **Reliable & Responsive:** The UI should feel fast and provide immediate feedback, reinforcing the "real-time" aspect of the application.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Ensure high contrast for text readability, clear focus states for keyboard navigation, and semantic HTML for screen reader compatibility. Avoid relying solely on color to convey information.
